See you in class!


Wu Dao is the premier Chinese martial arts school and training facility in the Greater Boston area. Located in Cambridge, MA, the school teaches Choy Lay Fut (Cai Li Fo) Kung Fu, Shaolin Kung Fu, and Yang Style Tai Chi (Tai Ji Quan) to adults and children, starting 6 years old. We offer classes six days a week, plus a special invitation only conditioning program on Sundays.
