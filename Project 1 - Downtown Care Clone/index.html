Practice Updates

Covid19 Vaccine Distribution: NYC has announced that certain groups, including people over age 65, are now eligible to receive the COVID19 vaccine. Please check your eligibility, and if eligible, sign up to receive the vaccine on the NYC DOH website below. Given the storage requirements and logistics of these vaccines, it is highly unlikely that smaller-private offices like ours will have the capability of getting these vaccines to our patients. We suggest signing up through your local health department. NYU Langone Hospital is not accepting referrals for patient vaccinations at this time. If you have additional questions/concerns, please call to set up a telehealth appointment with our office. https://www1.nyc.gov/site/doh/covid/covid-19-vaccine-eligibility.page

Appointments: Our office is open and back to regular hours for in-office physicals, routine follow ups, medication renewals, blood-work and routine vaccines! We will continue to provide Telemedicine for sick visits and certain follow up visits using our secure telehealth platform-curemd.securevideo.com, which can be accessed via your smart phone or computer. We are not performing diagnostic nasopharyngeal swabs for active COVID19 infections in the office, we can provide guidance if you need to get tested.

Safety Policies: Downtown Primary Care continues to follow CDC guidelines including increased disinfection procedures and appropriate protective gear and screening of providers/staff. We kindly ask that you respect the following policies. 1 You must wear a face mask in order to enter our building. Do not wear a mask with 1-way exhalation valves and keep your mask on during your entire visit. 2 Respect social distancing, maintaining a distance of 6 feet between you and our staff and doctors in the waiting area. 3 Do not bring children or other visitors with you to the office unless you require assistance. Anyone accompanying a patient must wait outside the office. 4 Patients’ temperatures will be taken upon arrival to the office. 5 If you or someone with whom you have had close contact has COVID19 or related symptoms such as fever, cough, shortness of breath, aches, or loss of taste or smell, do NOT come to the office. Also do NOT come to the office if you have traveled to any state on the NYS Travel Advisory List until after a 14 day quarantine.

General Information: Downtown Primary Care is a general internal medicine practice located in the Financial District. We founded our practice in 2002 on the belief that a primary care physician should be a “go to” resource for all of a person’s health needs. We are board certified internal medicine physicians with faculty appointments at the NYU School of Medicine.

Our practice uses a Patient Portal which is a secure site for patients to: Register, View clinical results, Request appointments, and Pay bills (click on $ Tab). Click on the Patient Portal Button above, which will take you to our Portal login page. Messages and requests are checked during Office Hours Only.

Call 212.379.6480 to schedule an appointment, existing patients can also use the portal to request appointments. Office hours are Monday – Friday from 9AM to 5PM.
